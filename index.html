Our History

Hot Beans Web was set up in 2019 by Murelina Havi because of her passion for designing websites. She came up with the idea of building her own company as she felt like companies couldnt work up to her expectations of websites, and so she wanted to create her own. In 2021, just after COVID-19 came along, Murelina decided to expand her business and hire some experts that do web developing and some IT technicians to help around. She invested her business and by 2022, she came from her small office in her home, to many offices around london, the biggest one yet being in Canary Wharf. This now allows her to expand and increase the amount of clientele we get. Now in 2024, Hot beans webs works is partnered up with mant companies, such as Amazon, Apple, Microsoft etc. We are always looking to improve and to grow so hopefully we have the right opportunities for you, so you can potentially join the HBW's family.
